10-12 Months Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 10-12 months, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 5 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Is able to call a parent by “dada” or “mama” or another special name

-Understands when you say “no” by stopping or pausing briefly

-Can wave “bye-bye” to others


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Can pull themselves up to stand

-Can walk with assistance by holding onto furniture

-As you hold it, they can drink from a cup without a lid

-Is able to pick things up (such as small bits of food) between their thumb and pointer finger
