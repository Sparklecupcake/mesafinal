1-2 Months Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 1-2 months, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 5 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Reacts to loud sounds

-Makes sounds similar to crying


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Moves their limbs

-Can keep their head up when on tummy

-Attempts to open hands
