3-4 Months Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 3-4 months, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 7 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Recognizes the sound of your voice by turning their head

-Makes cooing sounds (“aahh,” “oooo”)

-Responds with sounds when you speak to them


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Holds their head steady without aid when you hold them

-Can hold a toy when you put it in their hand

-Attempts to swing at toys using their arms

-Brings hands to their mouth

-Pushes themselves up onto their forearms or elbows when on tummy
