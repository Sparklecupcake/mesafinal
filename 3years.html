3 Years Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 3 years, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 6 or fewer checks. Additionally, your baby is due for a checkup! According to the AAP, the American Academy of Pediatrics recommends that your baby is due for general developmental screening. Please check with your pediatrician or doctor regarding this. What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Talks with you back and forth in a conversation

-Asks things like 'Where is Mommy?'

- Says what action is happening like 'eating,' or 'laying'

-Says their name

-Speech clear enough for others to occasionally understand


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Strings items such as maceroni

-Wears loose clothing by themselves

-Can use cutlery
