5-6 Months Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 5-6 months, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 6 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Can blow “raspberries” (can stick their tongue out and blow)

-Takes turns to make sounds with you

-Attempts or makes squealing noises


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Can roll from their tummy to their back independently

-Pushes themselves up from their tummy with straight arms

-When sitting, leans on their hands to support themselves
