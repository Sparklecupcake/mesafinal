2 Years Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 2 years, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 7 or fewer checks. Additionally, your baby is due for a checkup! According to the AAP, the American Academy of Pediatrics recommends that your baby is due for autism and general developmental screening. Please check with your pediatrician or doctor regarding this. What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Points to pictures in book when mentioned

-Says small 2 word phrases such as "Bless you"

-Points to body parts when mentioned

-Uses small gestures


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Runs around

-Kicks a ball around

-Walks up stairs

-Try to eat by theirselves
