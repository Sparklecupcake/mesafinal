2 Years Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 2 years, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 7 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Points to things in a book when you ask, like “Where is the bear?”

-Says at least two words together, like “More milk.”

-Points to at least two body parts when you ask them to show you

-Uses more gestures than just waving and pointing, like blowing a kiss or nodding yes


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Kicks a ball

-Runs

-Walks (not climbs) up a few stairs with or without help

-Eats with a spoon
