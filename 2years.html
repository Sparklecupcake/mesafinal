2 Years Milestone/Checklist:

If your baby is 2 years, here is a general checklist on what their progress should look like! At this age, consulting a doctor is recommended if your baby has 7 or fewer checks.


What MOST babies do by this age:


Language/Communication Milestones:

-Points to pictures in book when mentioned

-Says small 2 word phrases such as "Bless you"

-Points to body parts when mentioned

-Uses small gestures


Movement/Physical Development Milestones:

-Runs around

-Kicks a ball around

-Walks up stairs

-Try to eat by theirselves
